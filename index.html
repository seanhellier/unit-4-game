Crystal Collector Game

RULES

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. Clicking on a crystal will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  4. You lose if your total score goes above the random number.
  5. The value of each crystal is randomized at the start of each round.
